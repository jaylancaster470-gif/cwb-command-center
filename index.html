Our Story

A Legacy Carved
in Precious Stone

Founded in Beverly Hills in 1987 by master jeweler Henri Laurent, House of Laurent has spent nearly four decades defining what luxury jewelry truly means — not as adornment, but as legacy.

We source only the finest ethically certified diamonds, working directly with select mining operations in Botswana and Canada. Every stone is hand-selected, every setting inspected under magnification before leaving our atelier.

Today, House of Laurent serves a global clientele of discerning collectors, celebrities, and individuals who understand that the finest things in life are meant to endure.
