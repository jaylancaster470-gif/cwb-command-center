✅ Your Action Checklist

Decide: LLC vs Corporation

For most new business owners, an LLC is simpler, cheaper, and provides the same protection. This video explains both so you can make the right choice.

Choose your state of formation

You don't have to register in the state you live in. Learn which states have the best formation benefits for small businesses.

File your Articles of Organization

The official document that creates your LLC. You file this with your Secretary of State. Costs $50–$150 depending on the state. The video shows the exact form to fill out.

Get your Certificate of Formation

Once approved (1–5 business days), you receive this document. Save it. You'll need it to open a bank account, get your EIN, and apply for contracts.

Create an Operating Agreement

Even as a single-member LLC, you need this internal document. Some banks require it. A basic template is included in your checklist download.
